    All Livpower products are available on our site and can be enquired. Our Livpower team members will reach out to you and verify your order, pricing and itinerary of delivery

    In order to make an enquiry with Livpower, visit the Contact Us page or press the Bulk Enquiry button where there is the top right. Please fill your information and product needs and our Livpower team will reply to you with everything you need to know.

    Yes! Livpower provides delivery of all products at your place. It has installation services on selected products and locations. Livpower can offer a quick, secure and professional installation.

    Yes, Livpower offers standard warranty on all products but this can be subject to change depending on the model. To learn about the warranty of a particular product or to speak to one of the support agents, please refer to the product information on the Livpower site
